Conditions Treated

Our innovative physical therapy facility combines the restoration of bodily function by utilizing rehabilitation methods and wellness programs for an overall healthier approach to living.

  • Joint Pain
  • Acute and Chronic Spine Pain
    • Ex: Lumbar, Cervical, Thoracic
  • Spinal Conditions
    • Spinal Stenosis, Degenerative Disc Disease, Disc Herniation/Bulge, Scoliosis, Cervical/Lumbar Radiculopathy, Sacroiliac Joint Dysfunction
  • Post Surgical Care
    • Joint Replacements: Total Shoulder, Total Hip, Total Knee, Reverse Total Shoulder
    • Arthroscopic surgeries: Subacromial Decompression, Debridement, Menisectomy
    • Tendon Repairs: Rotator Cuff Repair, Bicep Tendon, ACL Repair, Peroneus Longus Repair
  • Post Mastectomy Lymphedema
  • Scar Tissue Dysfunction
  • Tendonitis and Bursitis
  • Car accident/Work Accident
  • Aches/Pains/Injuries
